Project Space is a simple game made by wintergrasped the goal of the game is not to get hit by the bouncing astroids. and acheive the highest score. You get 1 point every 10 seconds and 100 points every level you go up a level every 60seconds in the higher level you can regerate lives. the levels are progressivly harder.
